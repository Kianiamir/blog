From Zero to Git Pro - A Review of Colt Steele’s Bootcamp on Udemy

When I decided to learn data science, I started my learning path by diving into Git. My primary goal at this stage was to get familiar with the fundamentals. However, the amazing bootcamp by Colt Steele turned out to be much more than I ever imagined, and it’s the best programming course I’ve ever taken. The course consists of 20 chapters, and believe me, you won’t reach the last part easily. The course is super rich in content, quite lengthy, and requires proper note-taking and practice. For note-taking, I used Notion, which is my first, second, and third choice for taking notes, including some code snippets. If you’re not familiar with Notion, you can find plenty of YouTube channels and playlists to learn the basics in less than an hour.
